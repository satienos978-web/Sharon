ABOUT US

Tambua Afrika Studios is a creative house dedicated to producing edutainment content in:

African literature
Theatre
Comics
Film and animation.

Our aim:
To make African stories recognised, both locally and globally, delivering them in every form possible to reach audiences from all walks of life.

Founded by:
Ododa Emmanuel and Otieno Otenga
July 25, 2025.

MISSION & VISION

MISSION

To tell Africa's stories with authenticity and creativity, blending heritage with innovation, and empowering the next generation of artists through theatre, comics, film, and literary works.

VISION

To be Africa's leading creative house.
Producing world-class content that inspires, educates, and connects communities globally.
